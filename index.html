Chillin' in the Mall

In this section there is an interesting concept of layout breaking. On devices with a small screen, the elements in this section are adjusted to not break. However, in a marketing concept, the first element presented will be the "cheaper" and the tendency is for the user to choose it. To fix this, a 'media query' was done with 'max width' of a small device and then changed the order of the elements so that the most expensive comes first.
